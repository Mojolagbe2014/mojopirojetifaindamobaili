About this app

This mobile application eliminates the problems encountered with manual archive system of keeping academic research report in the University by substituting the manual system with electronic database. This gives Students, University community and the entire world that may have interest in the research that has been done in the university a quick access for the purpose of academic research or implementation. It store an academic research project report for every category of degree in University of Ilorin such as graduate project, postgraduate project and academic journals. Amoo Haruna Babatunde
